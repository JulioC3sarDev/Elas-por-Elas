Here you are protected

Elas por Elas was created with a single purpose: to ensure your safety. Here, you will find a welcoming space, made to listen, support, and guide. Every feature was designed so that you feel safe and supported in any situation.

Make quick reports

We know that in urgent moments, every second counts. That's why we offer a simple and fast way to file reports, with just a few clicks. The process is direct, secure, and efficient, ensuring that your voice is heard without complications.

Find police stations nearest to you

Easily find the police stations and support centers closest to your location. Our geolocation system helps you identify where to seek in-person help quickly and safely, always prioritizing your protection.

Report anonymously if you don't feel comfortable

You have the right to report without exposing yourself. If you prefer to keep your identity confidential, we offer the option of anonymous reporting. This way, you can help combat violence without putting your safety at risk.
