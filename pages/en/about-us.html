About the Elas por Elas Project

Connecting, protecting, and empowering women through technology.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer a safe and welcoming digital environment where women can find support, information, and resources to combat violence and ensure their safety. We believe that by joining forces and facilitating access to help, we can create an effective and supportive protection network.

Our Vision

We aim to be the main reference platform in Brazil for female safety, an ecosystem where technology acts as an indispensable ally in the prevention of violence and in the support of victims. We want a future where every woman feels safe to report, seek help, and live freely.

How it Works

The "Elas por Elas" platform centralizes essential security features. Users can make reports safely and confidentially, find and map the nearest police stations and support centers with detailed information, and access a support community. All just a touch away, in an intuitive and fast way.

Our Team

This project was conceived and developed with dedication by a team of students of Systems Analysis and Development from UNIT PE, committed to using technology to generate a positive social impact.
