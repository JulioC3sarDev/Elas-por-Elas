Help Center

Find answers to the most common questions about our platform.

Reports

How do I make a report?

To make a report, access your account and click on the "Report" card. Fill out the form with as much detail as possible and, if you have any, attach evidence such as photos, videos, or audio. If you do not want to identify yourself, use the "Anonymous Report" option on the home page.

Is my report truly anonymous?

Yes. When using the "Anonymous Report" page, none of your personal data is collected or sent with the report. Your security and privacy are our top priority.

What happens after I submit the report?

After submission, your report is forwarded to the competent partner agencies, who will analyze it and take the appropriate measures. The goal is to ensure that each case is treated with the seriousness it deserves.

Account and Security

Is my personal data secure?

Yes. We use encryption and the best data security practices to ensure that your personal information, stored in your account, remains confidential and protected against unauthorized access.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

On the login screen, click on the "Forgot password?" link. You will receive instructions in your email to create a new access password securely.

Support Stations

How do I find the nearest police station?

In your logged-in area, access the "Nearby Stations" feature. The platform will use your location to display a map with the police stations and support centers closest to you, along with contact information and address.

Need Immediate Help?

If you are in a dangerous situation, do not hesitate. Contact the emergency services.
